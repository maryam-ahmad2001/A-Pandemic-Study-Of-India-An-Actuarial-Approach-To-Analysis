# A-Pandemic-Study-Of-India-An-Actuarial-Approach-To-Analysis

My partners and I created two Actuarial Models to predict the Covid scenario of India for August 2020. We tried 

1. To analyze the existing statistical information at that time regarding the global status and experience under different conditions throughout the Pandemic.
2. We also tried To analyze and interpret the statistical models developed for projecting COVID-19 scenario of India. (Expected number of cases, deaths, recoveries)

We considered 5 relevant countries South Korea, Singapore, Italy, Germany, USA and studied the conditions there. Then we used the HIRD and SEIR models.
